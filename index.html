Systems

Design tokens as contracts

Tokens are promises between design and code. Treat them like APIs: version, lint, and deprecate.

Tokens bridge teams. I keep them in git, generate typings, and add contract tests in CI. When a token changes, a migration note ships with the PR and downstream apps get a changelog. Less drama, more shipping.

Performance

Latency as a UX feature

Users feel anything above 100ms. Design around it instead of apologizing with spinners.

I budget latency the way PMs budget dollars. Optimistic UI, prefetch, and skeletons keep the mind in flow. Metrics I track: TTFB, FID, CLS, and user-task success. Benchmarks belong in PRs, not afterthoughts.
